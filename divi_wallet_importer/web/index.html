This tool helps you import your 12-word mnemonic seed phrase from Divi Wallet Mobile into the Divi Desktop Application. It will guide you through every step of the recovery process.

Security: This tool runs on your local machine only. Your seed phrase never leaves your computer. The server is bound to 127.0.0.1 and all API calls require a session token.

Post-Recovery Checklist

  1. Be patient while Divi Desktop syncs and rescans
  2. When the “Encrypt Wallet” pop-up appears, close it (wait to encrypt for now)
  3. Check that your balance has been successfully migrated
  4. Close Divi Desktop completely
  5. Restart Divi Desktop
  6. Click the black triangle icon and set a password to encrypt your wallet
Once you have confirmed your balance, make sure to encrypt your wallet with a strong password.
